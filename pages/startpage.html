How to find-in-page

Alt + M

Summon or dismiss Mark My Search using the key combo above.

Toolbar

The toolbar will appear and stay on the page until dismissed.

Adding keywords to highlight

If the button is not open, press it to get a text field. After typing a keyword to highlight, tap Enter.

Page w/ highlighting

To close the text field, click somewhere else or tap Escape.

Editing keywords

Press to re-open the keyword's text field. Type anything and press Enter to change the keyword.

While the text field is open, you can press to bin the keyword.

Keyword in the toolbar, annotated

Highlight automatically when searching the web

Searching the web will summon Mark My Search automatically. All of your search terms will be highlighted, as if you had added them to the toolbar.
